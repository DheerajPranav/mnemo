### Quiz-me gate (logged)
1. *Design:* Why gate PII before extraction rather than redacting after? → So sensitive text never enters the pipeline at all — no row, no embedding, no log. Redaction-after leaves copies; gate-before leaves zero trace (I2).
2. *Edge case:* A fact says "budget is 60k as of 2026-04-30" — why isn't it PII? → The phone recognizer requires 3digit-SEP-4digit; "60k" and the 4-2-2 ISO date don't match, and no health/email/SSN token is present. Verified over the whole dataset (exactly m009 blocks).
3. *Change impact:* Someone loosens the phone regex to `\d{3}\d{4}`. → It would start matching order numbers / IDs → over-block legitimate facts → test_does_not_block_ordinary_gtm_facts fails, and M3's gate would lose real facts. The false-positive test is the guardrail.
