### Quiz-me gate (logged)
1. *Design:* Why resolve supersession on the write path, not by down-ranking stale facts at read time? → If it were a ranking signal, a sufficiently lexically-similar stale fact could still win (that's the baseline's F4). Removing it from the candidate set makes the failure unreachable, and adversarial probe A1 confirms even its exact text can't surface it.
2. *Edge case:* Two facts share (account, subject) and the same recorded_at (the SSO flip). Which wins? → The later ingestion `seq` (R1). Within a day, the later-recorded utterance supersedes; test_ranking.test_sso_tie_resolved_current_is_m006 locks it.
3. *Change impact:* Someone lowers the abstain threshold to 0.02 to "improve recall". → Cold-start queries stop abstaining (q09/q10 regress) and low-confidence facts get injected; gate G2 coldstart rate goes non-zero and the gate fails. The threshold + same-account rule are the guardrail.
